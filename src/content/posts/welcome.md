Welcome to **The Latent Space** — a place where I'll write about the things I'm
building, breaking, and rebuilding.

I just wrapped up my Master's in Computer Science at Portland State University,
and along the way I shipped a few systems I'm proud of: an API exploration
platform, a transit analytics pipeline, and an LLM-powered code documenter.
Each one taught me something I want to write down before I forget.

## What you'll find here

- Deep dives into projects I'm working on
- Notes on machine learning deployment and infra
- Short pieces on developer experience and tooling
- The occasional opinion on engineering culture

## Why blog at all

Writing forces clarity. Most of what I've learned in the last two years lives
in scattered notebooks and Slack threads. Moving it here makes it searchable —
both for me, and hopefully for someone else hitting the same wall.

More soon.
